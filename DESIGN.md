# Altimon — Game Design Document

## Concept
A 2D overhead RPG where the player captures and trains alien creatures 
called Altimons to stop a hostile faction from destroying the world.

## World
- A vague Earth-like planet, never explicitly named
- Geography: An archipelago of 5 islands, each with a distinct theme
- Altimons arrived via a comet storm from distant galaxies and have 
  been living on the planet ever since

## Story
The player is a customizable character (name + gender) living a normal 
life when they discover the faction's plan to weaponize Altimons and 
destroy the world. With no experience, they are taken under the wing of 
a retired Altimon researcher who gives them their first Altimon and 
teaches them the basics.

## Characters
### Player
- Name: Player chosen
- Gender: Player chosen
- Backstory: Ordinary kid, no prior Altimon training experience

### Mentor
- Role: Retired Altimon researcher
- Backstory: Studied the original comet storm, has deep knowledge 
  of Altimon origins
- Function: Gives player their first Altimon, explains game mechanics

### Antagonist Faction
- Name: The Eclipse Order
- Goal: Weaponize mind-controlled Altimons to destroy the world
- Motivation: Pure power — they want to rule, and destruction is their leverage
- Structure: Grunts → Scientists → Commander → Leader

## Starter Altimons

### Ignarok (Fire)
- Type: Fire
- Appearance: Small bipedal creature with a charcoal-colored rocky 
  body and a glowing molten core visible through cracks in its chest. 
  Two small magma horns sit on its head. Its tail ends in a flame 
  that changes color with its mood.
- Personality Traits (randomized): Brave, Reckless, Hotheaded, 
  Determined, Impulsive

### Tidalyx (Water)
- Type: Water
- Appearance: Four-legged creature resembling a cross between a fox 
  and a deep-sea fish. Translucent blue-white bioluminescent fur, 
  flowing fin-like ears, and a tail that splits into three fluid 
  tendrils. Large dark reflective eyes.
- Personality Traits (randomized): Calm, Cautious, Mysterious, 
  Loyal, Calculated

### Sporven (Grass)
- Type: Grass
- Appearance: Slender bipedal mantis-like creature with deep forest 
  green exoskeleton and bioluminescent vein patterns. Large scythe-like 
  forearms that extend in battle. Crystalline comet fragments sprouting 
  from its back. Compound eyes that glow amber.
- Personality Traits (randomized): Patient, Cunning, Observant, 
  Disciplined, Focused


## Type System
Uses the standard 18-type system:
Normal, Fire, Water, Grass, Electric, Ice, Fighting, Poison, 
Ground, Flying, Psychic, Bug, Rock, Ghost, Dragon, Dark, Steel, Fairy

Note: Type chart interactions are identical to standard RPG conventions.

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. Explore island
2. Encounter and capture wild Altimons
3. Battle trainers and faction members
4. Defeat island boss
5. Unlock next island
6. Uncover faction's plan through story beats
7. Final showdown on Island 5
