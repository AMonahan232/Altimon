# Altimon — Game Design Document

## Concept
A 2D overhead RPG where the player captures and trains alien creatures 
called Altimons to stop a hostile faction from destroying the world.

## World
- A vague Earth-like planet, never explicitly named
- Geography: An archipelago of 5 islands, each with a distinct theme
- Altimons arrived via a comet storm from distant galaxies and have 
  been living on the planet ever since

## Story
The player is a customizable character (name + gender) living a normal 
life when they discover the faction's plan to weaponize Altimons and 
destroy the world. With no experience, they are taken under the wing of 
a retired Altimon researcher who gives them their first Altimon and 
teaches them the basics.

## Characters
### Player
- Name: Player chosen
- Gender: Player chosen
- Backstory: Ordinary kid, no prior Altimon training experience

### Mentor
- Role: Retired Altimon researcher
- Backstory: Studied the original comet storm, has deep knowledge 
  of Altimon origins
- Function: Gives player their first Altimon, explains game mechanics

### Antagonist Faction
- Name: The Eclipse Order
- Goal: Weaponize mind-controlled Altimons to destroy the world
- Motivation: Pure power — they want to rule, and destruction is their leverage
- Structure: Grunts → Scientists → Commander → Leader

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. Explore island
2. Encounter and capture wild Altimons
3. Battle trainers and faction members
4. Defeat island boss
5. Unlock next island
6. Uncover faction's plan through story beats
7. Final showdown on Island 5
